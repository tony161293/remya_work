SILENT SPRING CHARITY AND ORGANIZATION

Silent Spring is a non – profitable charity organization founded during 2014 with the aim of helping single woman/single parent woman to lead a stable and happy life.

Silent Spring started on March 23,2014 in the memory of Smt. Gracy Issac , a science teacher by her daughters’ for supporting single woman and mother of single parent status in Kerala Society and helping them to lead a happy and fruitful life. This is an organization based on Christian principles and Values.

Vision: To give smile to the gloomy faces of single mother parent and single woman and enable them to lead a secure life financially and emotionally.

Mission: For the next three years

1. Database of single woman and single parent mother Kottayam, Pathanamthitta and Alappuzha districts

2. Grouping of beneficiaries

3. Meetings of beneficiaries

4. Chartering programmes

5. Identifcation of facilitators for groups
